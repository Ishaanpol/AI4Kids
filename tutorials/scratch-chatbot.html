Build a Chatbot in Scratch

Use Scratch blocks to make a helper that can introduce itself and answer simple questions.

Step 1 — Create the Sprite

  1. Start a new Scratch project. Delete the cat if you want.
  2. Choose a new sprite and background.
  3. Add a when green flag clicked block and say “Hi, I’m Chat-Bot!”

Step 2 — Ask & Answer

  1. Use ask [What’s your name?] and wait.
  2. Then say [Nice to meet you, (answer)!] for 2 seconds.
  3. Add if blocks to answer questions like “time” or “joke”.

Step 3 — Share

  1. Test your bot and fix any mistakes.
  2. Click Share to publish. Copy the link to show friends.
Tip: Keep answers kind and safe.
